ABOUT NUBE

Welcome to our NTC


NUBE Training Center is the flagship recreational and training center for NUBE members, trade unions within the country, region and international, governmental and non- governmental organizations and many student and religious organisations and the public. It is wholly owned by National Union of Bank Employees and its remains its prideful heritage to this very day. NTC established in 1975, was the vision of NUBE members; non-executive Bank workers then. The sense of unity and solidarity was so strong; they contributed one (1) month bonus payment to build their very own recreation facility.


The NTC houses a variety of accommodation ranging from private bedrooms to family/group chalets and affordable dorms larger groups for official and leisure. NTC has its very own NTC Café to tickle your taste buds.

NTC Café is proud of its reputation for tasty and affordable food with a variety of menus across the day which features traditional and healthy options to suit all tastes. Complementing the NTC Café is a lounge and bar terrace overlooking the vast blue sea. It is just perfect to relax and enjoy the windy sea breeze along with family and friend to savour mouth watering local and western delicacies twenty-four hours, seven days a week.

Alternatively take refuse at our Japanese Garden, one of the main attraction at The NTC to soothe your mind and calm your senses after a long business day or a fund day at the sea and enjoy a drink or two.

NTC also offer many opportunities for fun and games.
