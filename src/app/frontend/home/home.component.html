ABOUT US

NUBE Training Center is the flagship recreational and training center for NUBE members, trade unions within the country, region and international, governmental and non- governmental organizations and many student and religious organisations and the public. It is wholly owned by National Union of Bank Employees and its remains its prideful heritage to this very day. NTC established in 1975, was the vision of NUBE members; non-executive Bank workers then.
